Bottoms Pump

Follow these steps for the Bottoms Pump setup:

1. Bottoms Pump (Circulation Pump) - Location varies.

2. Get needed 5' of 2" rigid conduit. Install 3/4" conduit from panel rack to location of bottoms pump.

3. Install 3/4" flex from conduit to pump location.

4. Cut 2 pieces of unistrut at 5". Use 2 exhaust clamps to mount unistrut to leg of bottoms pump stand.

5. Cut 5' of 2" rigid conduit. Mount conduit on unistrut with 2" unistrut straps, exhaust clamps, pole needs holes.

6. Drill 4 holes in mounting brackets of bottoms pump panel. (Use exhaust clamps for hole reference) 2 holes on top and 2 holes on bottom.

7. Mount pump panel to pole using exhaust clamps.

8. Install 3/4" flex from the end of conduit run to the bottom of pump panel (brown/or/yel/grn).

9. Pull wire from power panel rack from flare disconnect to pump panel and terminate at the top of disconnect inside pump panel.

10. Install 3/4" flex from panel to pump motor on stand.

11. Pull #10 thn (brn/or/yel/green) from panel to motor and terminate. Panel wires terminate on the bottom of the overloads on motor starter. Motor wires terminate in accordance with nameplate.
