Salt Water Pump (SWP)

Follow these steps for the Salt Water Pump setup:

1. Salt Water Pump (SWP).

2. Install 1" conduit from saltwater pump disconnect to location of SWP VFD.

3. Install 1" flex from conduit to VFD panel.

4. Pull wire from disconnect on power panel rack to VFD panel and terminate wires on top side of disconnect breaker in panel.

5. Install 1" flex from VFD to SWP motor.

6. Pull wire from VFD to pump motor and terminate in accordance with nameplate on motor.

7. Terminate wires on the bottom of VFD (remove cover).
