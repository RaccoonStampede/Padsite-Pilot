Air Compressor

Follow these steps for the Air Compressor setup:

1. Air Compressor.

2. Install 3/4" conduit from air compressor disconnect to power panel rack to disconnect on air compressor skid.

3. Pull wire (#8/#8/#8/#10 thn) from disconnect to disconnect.

4. Install 3/4" flex from the end of conduit to the bottom of disconnect on skid.

5. Terminate wires on top side of disconnect on air compressor skid.
